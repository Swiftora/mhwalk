Mullica Hill Historic Walking Tour

Eat & Shop on Main Street

A guide to the village · South and North Main Street

Mullica Hill village is built around one main street. Almost everything below sits on South Main or North Main between the Old Eagle Hotel at the south end and the Old Mill complex at the north end, a comfortable walking spread of about half a mile. Confirm hours before you go. Listings change.

Eat, Drink, and Sweet Things

  • Naples at the Warehouse 2 South Main Street · east side Italian, full bar. Inside the historic warehouse at Stop 4 of this tour. Romeo family operation.
  • 322 BBQ & Butcher 1 South Main Street · west side, Old Mill complex Wood-smoked barbecue and a butcher counter, in the old grist mill.
  • Mullica Hill Donut Co. & Pancake House 1 South Main Street · west side, Old Mill complex Oversized donuts and a full breakfast menu.
  • blueplate 47 South Main Street · west side Farm-to-table New American by chef James Malaby. Breakfast and dinner.
  • La Mademoiselle Macaron 41 South Main Street · west side French macarons and pastries.
  • Cherry on Top Ice Cream & Candy Shoppe 26 South Main Street · east side Homemade ice cream, thirty-plus flavors of fudge, candy by the pound.
  • Amici Mullica Hill 69 North Main Street · west side Macarons, cakes, cupcakes, and custom dessert work.
  • Gather to Graze 69 North Main Street · west side Charcuterie boards and grazing tables, in the same building as Amici.
  • Harrison House Diner 98 North Main Street · east side Family-owned diner. A village landmark for more than thirty years. Open seven days.

Antiques and Vintage

  • The Old Mill Antique Mall 1 South Main Street · west side Multi-floor antique mall inside the eighteenth-century grist mill. Mullica Hill's largest antique destination, in operation since 1967.
  • Front Porch the Antiques 21 South Main Street · west side General antiques.
  • The Parsonage Antiques 31 South Main Street · west side Two-story, nine-room antique co-op with ten dealers.
  • Antiquities at Mullica Hill 43 South Main Street · west side Vintage farmhouse furniture, architectural pieces, interior design. Co-located with Trellis Antiques and Deeply Rooted Vintage Home.
  • The Yellow Garage Antiques 66 South Main Street · east side 6,500 square feet, thirty-five dealers, inside a 1922 former bus terminal across from Old Town Hall.
  • Old South Jersey Glass & Antiques 66 South Main Street · east side Specialist in early American blown glass, New Jersey glass, and eighteenth- and nineteenth-century pottery.
  • The Kinsey House 19 Woodland Avenue · one block east of Main An 1819 house with three floors of vintage, home and garden decor, and a carriage house out back.

Shops, Galleries, and Specialty

  • Brainstorm Books 41 South Main Street · west side, courtyard Independent bookstore. Fiction, non-fiction, children's, cards, coffee.
  • Farm House Design 41 South Main Street · west side, courtyard Farmhouse home and garden decor and furnishings.
  • Serendipity XIX 19 South Main Street · west side Two adjacent shops carrying women's clothing, accessories, gifts, and coastal home decor.
  • The Dressing Room on Main 34 South Main Street · east side Curated women's clothing, accessories, and gifts.
  • Sister's Fun to Fine Jewelry 39 South Main Street · west side Jewelry boutique with an on-site graduate gemologist. Twenty-plus years in town.
  • Mullica Hill Floral Co. 37 South Main Street · west side Sustainable florals, wedding and event work, local delivery.
