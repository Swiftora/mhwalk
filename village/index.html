Eat & Shop on Main Street

A guide to the village · South and North Main Street

The Mullica Hill village runs along South and North Main Street, most of it walkable in under half a mile. The businesses below are confirmed open as of May 2026. Tap a business name for walking directions in Google Maps. Tap "Website" for the shop's own page. Hours change. Call ahead for anything specific.

Eat, Drink, and Sweet Things

  • Naples at the Warehouse 2 South Main Street · east side Italian, full bar. Inside the historic warehouse at Stop 4 of this tour. Romeo family operation. Website ›
  • 322 BBQ and Red Barn Burgers 1 South Main Street · west side, Old Mill complex Wood-smoked barbecue and burgers, inside the old grist mill. Website ›
  • blueplate 47 South Main Street · west side Farm-to-table New American by chef James Malaby. Breakfast and dinner. Website ›
  • High Street Delicatessen 46 South Main Street · east side Sandwiches and creative deli plates, across from blueplate. Website ›
  • Bella Grace Coffee & Cafe 32 South Main Street · east side The village's only dedicated coffeehouse. Opened May 2025. Tuesday through Saturday. Website ›
  • La Mademoiselle Macaron 41 South Main Street · west side French macarons and pastries. Thursday through Saturday. Website ›
  • Cherry on Top Ice Cream & Candy Shoppe 26 South Main Street · east side Homemade ice cream, thirty-plus flavors of fudge, candy by the pound. Website ›
  • Amici Mullica Hill 69 North Main Street · west side Macarons, cakes, cupcakes, and custom dessert work. Website ›
  • Gather to Graze 69 North Main Street · west side, shared with Amici Charcuterie boards and grazing tables. Pre-order only, no walk-in hours. Website ›
  • Harrison House Diner 98 North Main Street · east side Family-owned diner. A village landmark for more than thirty years. Open seven days. Website ›

Antiques and Vintage

  • The Old Mill Antique Mall 1 South Main Street · west side Multi-floor antique mall inside the eighteenth-century grist mill. The village's largest antique destination, in operation since 1967. Open seven days. Website ›
  • The Parsonage Antiques 31 South Main Street · west side Two-story, nine-room antique co-op with ten dealers. Walk-in · tap name for directions
  • Antiquities at Mullica Hill 43 South Main Street · west side Vintage farmhouse furniture, architectural pieces, and interior design. Call (856) 478-6773
  • The Yellow Garage Antiques 66 South Main Street · east side, across from Old Town Hall 6,500 square feet, thirty-five dealers, inside a 1922 former bus terminal. Includes the Old South Jersey Glass showcase. Website ›

Shops, Galleries, and Specialty

  • Annie's Cranny 21 South Main Street · west side Women's clothing boutique. New tenant in the storefront formerly occupied by Front Porch Antiques. Hours vary · call ahead
  • Serendipity XIX 19 South Main Street · west side Two adjacent shops carrying women's clothing, accessories, gifts, and coastal home decor. Website ›
  • The Dressing Room on Main 34 South Main Street · east side Curated women's clothing, accessories, and gifts. Website ›
  • Sister's Fun to Fine Jewelry 39 South Main Street · west side Jewelry boutique with an on-site graduate gemologist. More than twenty years in town. Website ›
  • Mullica Hill Floral Co. 37 South Main Street · west side Sustainable florals, wedding and event work, local delivery. Website ›
