Privacy

This site is operated by the Harrison Township Historical Society, an all-volunteer 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. We respect your privacy and collect only minimal data to understand how the tour is used.

Analytics. We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymous usage data such as page views, navigation paths, and general device information. Google Analytics uses cookies. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Feedback form. The optional feedback form on each stop page is hosted by Google Forms. If you choose to submit a response, your answers are stored in the Society's Google account. The form does not collect your name or email address unless you voluntarily provide them.

Local storage. This site stores a small piece of data in your browser's local storage to remember which stop you last visited so you can resume the tour. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

No advertising or tracking. This site does not display advertising, does not sell or share data with third parties, and does not use tracking pixels or social media trackers.

Hosting. This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical information such as IP addresses in server logs as described in the GitHub Privacy Statement.

Contact. For questions about this policy, contact the Harrison Township Historical Society through their website at harrisonhistorical.com.
